Salsa

Simple and tasty red salsa

Ingredients

  • 28 oz can whole tomatoes including juice
  • 1 small white onion peeled and chopped
  • 1-2 jalapeno peppers seeded and chopped
  • 3 gloves garlic chopped
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cumin or to taste
  • 1 teaspon salt or to taste
  • 1/4 to 1 teaspoon granulated sugar optional and to taste
  • 1-2 handfuls cilantro
  • 3 tablespoons lime juice
  1. Add all ingredients to food processor in the order listed. Pulse a few times to break up the large pieces, then process until texture is as smooth as desired
  2. Taste the salsa and adjust as necessary based on personal preference
  3. Transfer to airtight contained and let sit in the refridgerator for a couple of hours or 1 day for better flavor
  4. Serve with tortilla chips and enjoy!
