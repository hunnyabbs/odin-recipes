Carne Asada Tacos

Carne Asada Taco plate

Ingredients

  • Carne Asada
  • Avocado
  • Onion
  • Lime
  • Cilantro
  • Cheese
  • Tortillas
  1. Grill the carne asada until it's cooked to your preference. Slice it against the grain, then chop into small pieces
  2. Mash the avocado and spread onto each tortilla
  3. Top the avocado with chopped carne asada, cotija cheese, diced onion, and fresh cilantro
  4. Squeee fresh lime juice on top of the tacos
